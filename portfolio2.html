Creating a GitHub Account
Why GitHub is Essential:

GitHub is the industry standard for version control and collaboration. It allows you to:

  • Showcase your projects to potential employers
  • Contribute to open source projects
  • Collaborate with other developers
  • Document your coding journey
Steps to Create Account:
  1. Visit github.com
  2. Click "Sign up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Create a secure password
  5. Choose a username (professional sounding recommended)
  6. Verify your email address
  7. Complete your profile with a photo and bio
Creating a LinkedIn Account
Why LinkedIn Matters:

LinkedIn is crucial for professional networking and career growth:

  • Connects you with industry professionals
  • Showcases your professional achievements
  • Provides job opportunities
  • Establishes your online professional presence
Steps to Create Account:
  1. Go to linkedin.com
  2. Click "Join now"
  3. Enter your email and create password
  4. Fill in your first and last name
  5. Complete your profile with:
    • Professional headline
    • Profile photo (professional)
    • Detailed work experience
    • Education history
    • Skills (at least 5)
  6. Connect with colleagues and classmates
