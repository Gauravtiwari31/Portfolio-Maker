How to Create a GitHub Account

  1. Go to github.com
  2. Click on "Sign up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email address, create a password, and choose a username
  4. Verify your account by solving the puzzle
  5. Choose your plan (Free is sufficient for most users)
  6. Complete the setup questions
  7. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your email
  8. Your GitHub account is now ready to use!

How to Create a LinkedIn Account

  1. Go to linkedin.com
  2. Click on "Join now"
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Fill in your first and last name
  5. Click "Agree & Join" to accept the terms
  6. Verify your account through the email sent to you
  7. Complete your profile by adding your location, current position, and education
  8. Add a professional profile photo
  9. Start connecting with colleagues and building your network
