How to Create a GitHub Account

  2. Click on "Sign up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email, create a username and password

How to Create a LinkedIn Account

  1. Go to linkedin.com
  2. Click on "Join now"
  3. Enter your name, email, and create a password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile with education and work experience
  6. Add a professional profile photo
  7. Start connecting with professionals in your field
