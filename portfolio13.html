How to Create a GitHub Account

  1. Go to github.com
  2. Click on "Sign up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email address, create a username and password
  4. Complete the verification process
  5. Choose your plan (Free is sufficient for most users)
  6. Complete your profile by adding a profile picture and bio
  7. Start creating repositories for your projects

GitHub is essential for showcasing your coding projects to potential employers and collaborators.

How to Create a LinkedIn Account

  1. Go to linkedin.com
  2. Click on "Join now"
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Complete your profile with your name and location
  5. Add your current or most recent position
  6. Upload a professional profile photo
  7. Add your education history
  8. Write a compelling headline and summary
  9. Start connecting with colleagues and classmates

LinkedIn is crucial for professional networking and job opportunities.
