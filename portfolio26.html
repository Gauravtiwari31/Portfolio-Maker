How to Create a GitHub Account

  1. Go to github.com
  2. Click on "Sign up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email address, create a username and password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete the setup by answering a few questions (optional)
  6. Start using GitHub to host your projects and collaborate with others

GitHub is essential for developers to showcase their work, contribute to open source, and collaborate on projects.

How to Create a LinkedIn Account

  1. Go to linkedin.com
  2. Click on "Join now"
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Fill in your first and last name
  5. Verify your email address
  6. Complete your profile by adding your education, experience, and skills
  7. Connect with professionals in your field

LinkedIn is the professional social network where you can showcase your career, connect with colleagues, and find job opportunities.
