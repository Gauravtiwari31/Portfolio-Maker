How to Create a GitHub Account

  1. Go to github.com
  2. Click on "Sign up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email address, create a username and password
  4. Complete the verification puzzle (it's fun!)
  5. Choose the free plan (perfect for artists)
  6. Upload your creative coding projects
  7. Share your animation experiments with the world!

How to Create a LinkedIn Account

  6. Upload a professional profile picture (a sketch is fine!)
  7. Connect with other animators and studios
