How to Create a GitHub Account

  1. Go to github.com
  2. Click on "Sign up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email address, create a password, and choose a username
  4. Complete the verification process
  5. Choose your plan (Free is sufficient for most users)
  6. Complete your profile by adding a photo and bio
  7. Start creating repositories for your projects

GitHub is essential for showcasing your coding projects and collaborating with other developers.

How to Create a LinkedIn Account

  1. Go to linkedin.com
  2. Click on "Join now"
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Fill in your first and last name
  5. Complete the verification process
  6. Add your current position and education
  7. Upload a professional profile photo
  8. Write a compelling headline and summary
  9. Start connecting with professionals in your field

LinkedIn is crucial for professional networking and job opportunities.
